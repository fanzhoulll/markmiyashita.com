Recent Projects

My most recent project has been Appsearch.es, an iOS app search engine that allows you to filter and sort through various iOS apps. The most interesting part about the project was making a worker that would collect data every night so that the database was always up to date. I am currently tracking the positions of the top 300 apps and I hope to be able to use that data in a couple weeks to measure the performance of certain types of apps on the App Store.
